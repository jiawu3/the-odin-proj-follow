Buttery mashed potatos are a great comfort food for a cold day, plus potatoes are incredibly affordable

Below is a very simplistic recipe

  • Potatos
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Masher
  • Butter
  1. Boil water with some salt and throw potatos in
  2. Once soft enough to poke through with a fork dump into a bowl and mash
  3. Put butter, salt and pepper and mix
